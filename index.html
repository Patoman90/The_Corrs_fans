The Corrs

Introducing the Corrs

The Corrs are and Irish rock pop band who combine traditional Irish instruments, rock and vocals together to create a unique flavour of music they are known for. The band is made up of siblings Sharon, Caroline, Andrea and Jim. They set up the band in the 1990's and released alot of their own music as well as covers of other songs. The band is from Dundalk county Louth in Ireland. The band has been active since 1991 and took a break in 2006. After they had established their own families they reunited in 2016 and have been active since. They have a broad fanbase in Ireland and Europe and the rest of the world. They have played at a variety of venues and events including the Special Olymics. The band members themselves too have taken the time to help out with a variety of charities.

Meet the Corrs

Andrea Jane Corr

Andrea Corr is the lead singer of the band. She is the youngest member and sister born on 17th May 1974. She does lead vocals and plays the tin whistle in some of their music. She has a very natural look about her and has always kept herself modest while being able to rock her beauty. She is married and has kids of her own. She has taken part in charity work and did some acting and solo songs too. She has been given a honorary MBE for her contributions to music and charity.
